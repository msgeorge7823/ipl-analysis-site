Legacy

The Gujarat Lions were one of two temporary franchises (alongside Rising Pune Supergiant) created to fill the void left when Chennai Super Kings and Rajasthan Royals were suspended for two years following the 2013 spot-fixing and betting scandal.

Led by Suresh Raina — who had captained CSK's feeder teams — the Lions had an impressive debut season in 2016, finishing in the top 4 and reaching the playoffs. However, their second and final season in 2017 was a disappointment, finishing last.

Many of their key players (Raina, Jadeja, Bravo) returned to CSK when the suspension ended in 2018. Gujarat would later get a permanent franchise in 2022 with the Gujarat Titans.

Replaced by: Rajasthan Royals (returned 2018)
