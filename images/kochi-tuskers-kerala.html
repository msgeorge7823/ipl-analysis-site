Legacy

Kochi Tuskers Kerala holds the distinction of being the shortest-lived IPL franchise — playing only a single season in 2011. They represented Kerala, the only time the state has had an IPL team. The team finished 8th out of 10 in their sole season. The franchise was terminated before IPL 2012 due to ownership consortium financial disputes and failure to meet BCCI's bank guarantee requirements. Their story remains a cautionary tale about IPL franchise financial governance.
