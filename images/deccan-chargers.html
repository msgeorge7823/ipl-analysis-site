Legacy

The Deccan Chargers were one of the 8 original IPL franchises. Under Adam Gilchrist's captaincy, they won IPL 2009, becoming champions after finishing last in the inaugural 2008 season — the greatest turnaround in IPL history. The franchise was terminated in 2012 due to financial irregularities and breach of contract. Their Hyderabad base was taken over by Sunrisers Hyderabad in 2013. Rohit Sharma began his IPL career here before moving to Mumbai Indians.
