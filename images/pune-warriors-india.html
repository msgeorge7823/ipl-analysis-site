Legacy

Pune Warriors India were one of two new franchises (along with Kochi Tuskers Kerala) added for IPL 2011, expanding the league to 10 teams. Despite having marquee names like Yuvraj Singh and Sourav Ganguly as captains, the team never found success on the field — finishing 9th in 2011, 9th in 2012, and 9th again in 2013. The franchise withdrew from the IPL after 2013 following protracted disputes with the BCCI over the reduced franchise fee when the league shrank from 10 to 8 teams. Steve Smith played some of his earliest T20 cricket here before becoming an international star.
