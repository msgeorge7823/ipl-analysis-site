Legacy

Rising Pune Supergiant is perhaps the most fascinating temporary franchise in IPL history. Created alongside Gujarat Lions as a replacement for suspended Chennai Super Kings, the team featured MS Dhoni playing AWAY from CSK for the first (and only) time.

In 2016, under Dhoni's captaincy, the team struggled. But in 2017 — with Steve Smith replacing Dhoni as captain and Ben Stokes' record-breaking signing — they reached the IPL Final, losing to Mumbai Indians by just 1 run in one of the greatest finals ever played.

The RPSG Group (owner Sanjiv Goenka) would later return to the IPL as owners of the Lucknow Super Giants in 2022.
